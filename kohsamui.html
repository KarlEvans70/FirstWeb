Koh Samui

There's a lot visitors like about Koh Samui, especially its beaches and restaurants. Enjoy fun activities like swimming and snorkelling. Collect beach glass at top spots like Lamai Beach and Chaweng Beach. Nathon Pier and Bo Phut Beach are other popular sights to visit.
